New Hindustan Tubes

In the steel tube and pipe manufacturing sector, New Hindustan Tubes has become a name that embraces precision, strength, and reliability. Having a deep-rooted tradition of providing excellence, we have established ourselves as one of the most reliable manufacturers and suppliers of premium M.S. Oxygen Lancing Pipes, ERW Steel Tubes, M.S. Pipes, Draw Pipes, Profile Pipes, CDW Pipes, and bespoke piping solutions.

We have stayed dedicated to innovation, quality craftsmanship, and customer-focused services since we began. Our offerings serve a diverse range of sectors, such as steel manufacturing, construction, infrastructure development, automotive, mechanical engineering, and fabrication, to name a few.

New Hindustan Tubes has a straightforward mission: to provide products that go beyond what customers expect, grateful to exceptional quality, punctual delivery, and excellent service.
